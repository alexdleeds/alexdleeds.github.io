Freewheel is an adventure in brewing

Freewheel Brewing Company was established in 2012 with a mission to fuse English tradition and American audacity into a collection of beers that are full-flavored yet balanced. If you’d like to try our beer from the source, visit our pub for a pint in Redwood City, CA.
